When veterans return home from service, it is beneficial for them to have activities to participate in as they adjust to a civilian life.

Phalanx Outdoors is proud to help and support our veterans. By allowing us to hold events on your property, you are providing verterans the ability to participate in outdoor activities which help provide an easier transition. Please partner with us to help veterans by authorizing access to your property for hunts, donating supplies, or by volunteering for events.
