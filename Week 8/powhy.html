Continuing the Mission

Why We Hunt

Currently it is estimated that 20 veterans commit suicide a day. We belive that part of this reason is due to the difficulty to transitioning to a civilian lifestyle. We hold our hunting events in order provide veterans an outlet to meet others in similar situations while staying active.

How We Began

Phalanx Outdoors began after our founders transitioned out of the military. They noticed that the lives they once knew would never return to normal. Hunting and other outdoor activities provided them the ability to regain some normalcy. We wanted to share what we found with other veterans in hopes of giving them a next mission.
